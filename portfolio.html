My Projects

3D Printing

I started 3d printing during the pandemic in 2020, in response to shortage of masks in my area. As part of a group effort with local 3D printers, we were able to donate sturdy mask to essentail personnel. When masks became readily available, I focused on upgrading my 3D printer. A connected Raspberry Pi allowed for starting jobs remotely and I was able to connect a webcam for monitoring and recording
